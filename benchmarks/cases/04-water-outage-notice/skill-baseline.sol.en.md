# Water Outage Notice

Residents of Sakura Heights Buildings A and B:

Water service will be unavailable while the supply pump is replaced.

**Date:** Tuesday, July 21, 2026  
**Time:** 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.

Service may return earlier if the work is completed ahead of schedule.

Before the outage, please store enough water for your needs and make sure all taps are closed. Do not use washing machines or dishwashers during the outage.

Immediately after service returns, the water may contain air or appear cloudy. Please run cold water for two to three minutes before using it. If the water does not clear, contact the management office.

Residents who need help preparing water because of age, disability, or another reason should contact the management office by Friday, July 17.

**Management Office:** 03-1234-5678  
**Office hours:** 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

We apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
