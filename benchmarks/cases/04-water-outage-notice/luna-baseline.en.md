# Water Outage Notice

Dear Residents,

Water service will be unavailable in Buildings A and B of Sakura Heights on Tuesday, July 21, 2026, from 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. while the supply pump is replaced. Service may resume earlier if the work is completed ahead of schedule.

Please:

- Store enough water for your needs before the outage.
- Check that all taps are fully closed.
- Do not use washing machines or dishwashers during the outage.

After service resumes, the water may initially appear cloudy or contain air. Run cold water for two to three minutes before using it. If it does not clear, please contact the management office.

Residents who need assistance preparing water because of age, disability, or another reason should contact the management office by Friday, July 17, 2026.

Management Office: 03-1234-5678  
Hours: 9:00 a.m.–5:00 p.m.

We apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your understanding.
