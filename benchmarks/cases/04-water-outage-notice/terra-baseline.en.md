# Notice of Water Outage

Dear Residents of Sakura Heights Buildings A and B,

Water service will be unavailable on **Tuesday, July 21, 2026, from 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.** while the supply pump is replaced. Service may return earlier if the work is completed ahead of schedule.

Please store the water you will need in advance and make sure all taps are closed before the outage begins. Do not use washing machines or dishwashers during the outage.

When water service returns, air or cloudiness may appear in the water. Please run cold water for two to three minutes before using it. If the water does not clear, contact the management office.

Residents who need help preparing water because of age, disability, or another reason should contact the management office by **July 17**.

We apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
