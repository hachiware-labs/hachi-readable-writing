# Water Outage Notice

Residents of Sakura Heights Buildings A and B:

Water service will be unavailable on Tuesday, July 21, 2026, from 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. while the supply pump is replaced. Service may return earlier if the work is completed ahead of schedule.

Before the outage, please store the water you will need and make sure all taps are closed. Do not use washing machines or dishwashers while the water is off.

When service returns, air or cloudiness may appear in the water. Please run cold water for two to three minutes before using it. If the water does not clear, contact the management office.

If you need assistance preparing water because of age, disability, or another reason, please contact the management office by July 17.

Management Office: 03-1234-5678  
Hours: 9:00 a.m.–5:00 p.m.

We apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your cooperation.
