# Water Outage Notice

Dear Residents of Sakura Heights,

Water service will be unavailable in Buildings A and B on Tuesday, July 21, 2026, from 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. The outage is necessary to replace the supply pump. Service may resume earlier if the work is completed ahead of schedule.

Before the outage:

- Store enough water for your needs.
- Check that all taps are fully closed.
- Arrange assistance in preparing water if needed.

During the outage, please do not use washing machines or dishwashers.

After service resumes, the water may initially appear cloudy or contain air. Run cold water for two to three minutes before using it. If the water does not clear, please contact the management office.

Residents who need help preparing water because of age, disability, or another reason should contact the management office by July 17.

Management Office: 03-1234-5678  
Hours: 9:00 a.m.–5:00 p.m.

We apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your understanding.
