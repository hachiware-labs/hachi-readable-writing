# Case 04: Water outage notice

The document is a notice for residents delivered both on paper and by email. It must make the event, preparation, recovery action, and assistance route understandable to people with different levels of attention and technical knowledge.

The comparison should check whether the most urgent facts are easy to find, whether the reason and apology create an appropriate tone, and whether concise formatting still preserves a readable progression.
