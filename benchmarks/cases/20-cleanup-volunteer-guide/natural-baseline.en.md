# Volunteers Needed: Midori Park Cleanup

Help keep our neighborhood park clean and welcoming!

**Date:** Sunday, November 8, 2026  
**Time:** 8:30–11:00 a.m.  
**Meeting place:** East entrance of Midori Park  
**Rain date:** Sunday, November 15

Volunteers will collect litter, gather leaves, and sort the collected material. Organizers will provide work gloves, trash bags, and litter tongs.

## Schedule

- **8:30 a.m.** — Check-in and safety briefing
- **8:45 a.m.** — Team cleanup begins
- **10:30 a.m.** — Sorting and activity report
- **11:00 a.m.** — Event ends

## What to Bring

Please wear closed-toe shoes and weather-appropriate clothing, and bring drinking water.

Participants aged 15 or older may attend alone. Anyone under 15 must attend with a parent or guardian.

For your safety, do not touch needles, chemical containers, large pieces of broken glass, or other hazardous material. Notify your team leader instead. Volunteers must not enter the road or riverbank.

## How to Register

Register by **Thursday, November 5**, by emailing [clean@example.org](mailto:clean@example.org) with:

- The participant’s name
- Phone number
- The accompanying guardian’s name, if the participant is under 15

If the cleanup is postponed because of rain, registered participants will be notified by email by **7:00 a.m. on November 8**.

Information about insurance, parking, and participation benefits is not available.
