# Volunteer Recruitment: Midori Park Cleanup

Help care for our neighborhood park on Sunday, November 8, from 8:30 to 11:00 a.m. We will meet at the east entrance of Midori Park.

If it rains, the cleanup will move to Sunday, November 15. Registered participants will receive an email by 7:00 a.m. on November 8 if the event is postponed.

## What we will do

We will collect litter, gather leaves, and sort the collected material for disposal.

- 8:30 a.m. — Check-in and safety briefing
- 8:45 a.m. — Team cleanup begins
- 10:30 a.m. — Sorting and activity report
- 11:00 a.m. — Event ends

Organizers will provide work gloves, trash bags, and litter tongs. Please bring closed-toe shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, and drinking water.

## Safety and participation

People aged 15 or older may participate on their own. Participants under 15 must attend with a parent or guardian.

Do not touch needles, chemical containers, large broken glass, or other hazardous material. Notify the team leader instead. Please do not enter the road or riverbank.

## How to register

Register by Thursday, November 5, by emailing [clean@example.org](mailto:clean@example.org). Include:

- Your name
- Your phone number
- If a participant is under 15, the accompanying parent or guardian’s name
