# Volunteers Needed: Midori Park Cleanup

Join your neighbors in cleaning Midori Park by collecting litter, gathering leaves, and sorting the collected material.

**Date:** Sunday, November 8, 2026  
**Time:** 8:30–11:00 a.m.  
**Meeting place:** East entrance of Midori Park  
**Rain date:** Sunday, November 15

## Register by November 5

Email `clean@example.org` by **Thursday, November 5**, with:

- The participant’s name
- A phone number
- The accompanying parent’s or guardian’s name if the participant is under 15

People aged 15 or older may participate alone. Anyone under 15 must attend with a parent or guardian.

If the cleanup is postponed because of rain, registered participants will be notified by email by **7:00 a.m. on November 8**.

## Schedule

- **8:30 a.m.:** Check-in and safety briefing
- **8:45 a.m.:** Cleanup begins in teams
- **10:30 a.m.:** Sorting and activity report
- **11:00 a.m.:** Event ends

Organizers will provide work gloves, trash bags, and litter tongs. Please wear closed-toe shoes and weather-appropriate clothing, and bring drinking water.

## Safety

Do not touch needles, chemical containers, large pieces of broken glass, or other hazardous material. Notify your team leader instead. Participants must not enter the road or riverbank.

Information about insurance, parking, and participation benefits is not currently available.
