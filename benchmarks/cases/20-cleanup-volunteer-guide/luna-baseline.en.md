# Volunteers Wanted: Midori Park Cleanup

Help keep our neighborhood park clean!

## Event Details

**Date:** Sunday, November 8, 2026  
**Time:** 8:30–11:00 a.m.  
**Meeting place:** East entrance of Midori Park  
**Rain date:** Sunday, November 15, 2026

## How to Register

Email **clean@example.org** by **Thursday, November 5** with:

- Participant’s name
- Phone number
- Accompanying parent or guardian’s name, if the participant is under 15

If the event is postponed, registered participants will receive an email notice by **7:00 a.m. that day**.

## Schedule

- **8:30 a.m.:** Check-in and safety briefing
- **8:45 a.m.:** Team cleanup begins
- **10:30 a.m.:** Sort collected material and begin the activity report
- **11:00 a.m.:** Event ends

Cleanup activities include collecting litter, gathering leaves, and sorting collected material.

Organizers will provide work gloves, trash bags, and litter tongs. Please bring closed-toe shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, and drinking water.

## Safety and Age Requirements

Participants aged 15 or older may attend alone. Anyone under 15 must attend with a parent or guardian.

Do not touch needles, chemical containers, large broken glass, or other hazardous material. Notify the team leader instead. Do not enter the road or riverbank.
