# Volunteers Wanted: Midori Park Cleanup

Help clean up Midori Park on Sunday, November 8, 2026, from 8:30 to 11:00 a.m.

If it rains, the cleanup will move to Sunday, November 15. A postponement notice will be emailed to registered participants by 7:00 a.m. that day.

## Where to meet

Meet at the east entrance of Midori Park.

## What we will do

Volunteers will:

- Collect litter
- Gather leaves
- Sort the collected material

## Schedule

- 8:30 a.m. — Check-in and safety briefing
- 8:45 a.m. — Team work begins
- 10:30 a.m. — Sorting and activity report
- 11:00 a.m. — Event ends

## What to bring

Organizers will provide work gloves, trash bags, and litter tongs.

Please bring:

- Closed-toe shoes
- Weather-appropriate clothing
- Drinking water

People aged 15 or older may participate alone. Anyone under 15 must attend with a parent or guardian.

## Safety

Do not touch needles, chemical containers, large broken glass, or other hazardous material. Notify your team leader instead.

Do not enter the road or riverbank.

## How to register

Register by Thursday, November 5, by emailing [clean@example.org](mailto:clean@example.org) with:

- The participant’s name
- The participant’s phone number
- For anyone under 15, the accompanying guardian’s name

Information about insurance, parking, and participation benefits is not specified in this notice.
