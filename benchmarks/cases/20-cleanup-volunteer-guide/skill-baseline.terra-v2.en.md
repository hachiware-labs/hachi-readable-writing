# Volunteer Recruitment: Midori Park Cleanup

Help care for our neighborhood park on Sunday, November 8, from 8:30 to 11:00 a.m. We will meet at the east entrance of Midori Park to collect litter, gather leaves, and sort the material we collect.

If it rains, the cleanup will move to Sunday, November 15. Registered participants will receive an email by 7:00 a.m. that day if the event is postponed.

## Register by Thursday, November 5

Email `clean@example.org` with:

- Your name
- Your phone number
- If a participant is under 15, the accompanying parent or guardian’s name

Participants aged 15 or older may join on their own. Participants under 15 must attend with a parent or guardian.

## What to bring

Organizers will provide work gloves, trash bags, and litter tongs. Please bring closed-toe shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, and drinking water.

## Schedule

- **8:30 a.m.** Check-in and safety briefing
- **8:45 a.m.** Team cleanup begins
- **10:30 a.m.** Sorting and activity report
- **11:00 a.m.** Event ends

For everyone’s safety, do not touch needles, chemical containers, large broken glass, or other hazardous material. Notify a team leader instead. Please also stay out of the road and away from the riverbank.
