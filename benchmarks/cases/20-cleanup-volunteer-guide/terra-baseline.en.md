# Volunteers Needed: Midori Park Cleanup

Join your neighbors on **Sunday, November 8, 2026, from 8:30 to 11:00 a.m.** to help keep Midori Park clean and welcoming. We will meet at the **east entrance of Midori Park**.

Volunteers will collect litter, gather leaves, and sort collected material. Work gloves, trash bags, and litter tongs will be provided. Please wear closed-toe shoes and weather-appropriate clothing, and bring drinking water.

## Schedule

- **8:30 a.m.** Check-in and safety briefing  
- **8:45 a.m.** Team cleanup begins  
- **10:30 a.m.** Sorting and activity report  
- **11:00 a.m.** Event ends  

Participants aged 15 or older may attend on their own. Children under 15 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

For everyone’s safety, do not touch needles, chemical containers, large broken glass, or other hazardous material. Tell your team leader if you find any of these items. Please stay out of the road and off the riverbank.

## How to Register

Register by **Thursday, November 5** by emailing [clean@example.org](mailto:clean@example.org). Include:

- Participant’s name  
- Phone number  
- Accompanying parent or guardian’s name, if the participant is under 15  

If rain requires postponement, the cleanup will take place on **Sunday, November 15**. Registered participants will receive an email by **7:00 a.m.** on the event day. Parking and insurance information has not been provided.
