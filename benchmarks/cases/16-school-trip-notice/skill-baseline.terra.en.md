Our fourth-grade students will visit the Municipal Science Museum on Friday, October 16, 2026.

- A packed lunch
- A water bottle
- A hat
- Rainwear
- A pencil and notebook

If your child takes regular medication, please submit the designated medication notice to the teacher by Friday, October 9.

If severe weather requires us to cancel the trip, families will receive a notice through the school communication app by 6:30 a.m. on October 16.

For questions, please contact the fourth-grade teachers at 03-5555-0104 before 4:30 p.m. on weekdays.
