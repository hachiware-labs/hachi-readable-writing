Create a presentation script introducing our prototype equipment-inventory app to the General Affairs team.
The presentation, including a live demonstration, should take about ten minutes.

What we know:

- Equipment at four sites is currently managed in a separate Excel workbook for each site.
- General Affairs combines the files at month end, taking about six hours every month.
- Inconsistent item names and missed updates contributed to 37 differences between records and physical inventory in the last count.
- The prototype stores asset number, item name, site, storage location, condition, and last verification date.
- A smartphone can scan the asset-number QR code and update condition and location.
- Users can filter by site and by items not yet verified.
- History records who made an update, when, and the before-and-after values.
- The demonstration should scan a QR code, change a storage location, and show the item disappearing from the unverified list.
- The prototype does not handle purchase requests, depreciation, or integration with the external accounting system.
- It contains only 30 sample records, and performance testing has not been done.
- We want a one-month trial with about 500 items on the head-office floor.
- During the trial, we will record registration time, inventory-count time, discrepancy count, and user questions.
- General Affairs should approve cooperation with the trial and appoint one person to prepare the equipment data.
