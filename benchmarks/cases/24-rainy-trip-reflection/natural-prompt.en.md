Write a reflection on a day trip to Kamakura that did not go according to plan.
It is a travel story to share with the friends who went.

What we know:

- Three friends from university went out together for the first time in ten years.
- We met at Kamakura Station at 9:00 a.m. on June 14.
- The plan was to walk along the coast and visit two temples.
- Heavy rain began just after we met, and one friend developed sore feet from new shoes.
- We waited for the rain to ease in a café near the station.
- At first everyone felt disappointed after making the trip.
- As we began catching up, we talked about things that had not come up in messages: changing jobs, caring for a parent, and a child's schooling.
- We stayed in the same café until after noon, and the server refilled our hot water several times.
- The rain eased in the afternoon, and we visited just one nearby temple.
- The wet hydrangeas were beautiful, and the grounds were quiet.
- On the way home we agreed not to over-plan next time and to meet again within a year.
- The story should retain both the disappointment of missing the sights and the value of having time to talk.
