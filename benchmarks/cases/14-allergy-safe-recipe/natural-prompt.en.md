I want to make chickpea and vegetable curry for five within 30 minutes on a weeknight.
Write a recipe an inexperienced cook can make in one deep skillet. One family member is vegetarian, and another has a severe peanut allergy.

What we know:

- Use two cans of chickpeas, one onion, two carrots, one red bell pepper, one can of chopped tomatoes, 400 ml vegetable stock, 150 g spinach, one tablespoon vegetable oil, two tablespoons curry powder, and salt.
- Cooked rice is already available separately.
- Do not use meat, fish, dairy, or eggs.
- Before cooking, check that every packaged ingredient, including curry powder, stock, and canned goods, contains no peanuts and has no relevant precautionary label.
- Wash the work surface, pan, knife, cutting board, and utensils. Do not share utensils or oil previously used with peanuts.
- Do not describe the meal as absolutely safe.
- The planned cooking sequence is to sauté the onion, carrots, and pepper; add curry powder, tomatoes, stock, and chickpeas; simmer; then add spinach at the end.
